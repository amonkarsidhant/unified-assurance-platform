# Self-Reflection Block (Required for major design docs/reports)

Use this block near the end of the document.

## Self-Reflection

- **Objective clarity:** What problem are we solving, and for whom?
- **Alternatives considered:** What did we reject, and why?
- **Risk assessment:** What can go wrong (security/compliance/operability)?
- **Trade-offs accepted:** What did we optimize for vs defer?
- **Validation evidence:** What tests/checks support this decision?
- **Follow-up triggers:** What signals would cause us to revisit this decision?
- **Owner + review date:** Who owns this, and when is it reviewed next?
